FusionFall Re:Spawn was cancelled in December of 2021. It was using the Unity's game engine like the original. I joined the development team in hope of creating something that people will be able to enjoy as much as I enjoyed playing the original game as a kid back in 2012-2013.

I worked on multiple scripts for FusionFall Re:Spawn such as the main menu scripts and the player camera and movement scripts, while also helping others on the team by providing informational resources from Unity's scripting documentation.

Game-bot

Work in progress - Play and level up to unlock special profile card backgrounds, titles and badges to have the most unique card out there.

Discord TV

There is no Discord app for Android TV so I decided to make my own worked out the QR authentication process by looking at the minified javascript from the desktop client and ported the code over to kotlin and implemented it into the app, before realizing the app would be breaking Discord's ToS and missing functionality available only to users with no information in Discord's documentation so I ended up quitting.
